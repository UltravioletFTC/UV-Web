About Us

Our Purpose

Each year our team members exhibit their innovative minds by creating a robot that scores points in a competitive environment. These experiences with those from our team-guided volunteering events create a unified team that embodies the FIRST's goal of empowering future STEAM leaders; For the Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology.

  • Creating a completely custom robot.
  • Volunteering time and resources to our community.
  • Empowering future STEAM leaders.
